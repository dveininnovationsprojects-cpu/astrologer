Frequently Asked Questions

Your questions, answered. Find clarity on our process and what to expect.

What do I need for a reading? expand_more

For an accurate birth chart reading, you will need your exact date, time, and city of birth. If you don't know your exact birth time, we can still perform a reading, but some details may be less specific.

How are readings delivered? expand_more

Readings are conducted live via a video call (Zoom or Google Meet) to allow for an interactive session where you can ask questions. You will also receive a recording of the session and a written summary of the key insights afterward.

Is astrology a form of fortune-telling? expand_more

Astrology is not about predicting a fixed future. Instead, it's a tool for self-awareness and understanding cosmic energies and potentials. It empowers you to make conscious choices, navigate challenges, and align with your highest path.
